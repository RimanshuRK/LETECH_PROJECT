About Us

About Us LETECH is a pioneering geospatial technology company, leading global innovation through advanced solutions in Remote Sensing (RS), Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Photogrammetry, LiDAR, and Drone and Ground Surveys. Since our inception in 2021, we have rapidly established ourselves as a trusted partner for governments, industries, and organizations worldwide. ​
​
​
Headquartered in the vibrant city of Hyderabad, India, LETECH is driven by a team of highly skilled professionals with unmatched domain expertise and vast experience. Our commitment to excellence and cutting-edge technology makes us a beacon of reliability and innovation in the geospatial industry.

At LETECH, we aspire to create a world where geospatial technology breaks barriers, empowering everyone to make informed decisions with ease. Our goal is to unlock the vast potential of geospatial data to address complex global challenges, from environmental sustainability and urban development to disaster management and beyond. By pushing the boundaries of innovation, we aim to shape a smarter, more connected world.

Our mission is to equip our clients with exceptional geospatial insights and solutions that fuel progress and growth. We are committed to advancing innovation, utilizing cutting-edge technology, and partnering with industry leaders to redefine how we understand and engage with our planet. By delivering impactful solutions, we aim to drive sustainable success for our clients and the world.
